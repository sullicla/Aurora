Capturing Data on Your Device
We use our phone and it collects information

The first step in the process is capturing the data on your phone. This could be anything from sending a text message, making a phone call, or browsing the internet. When you perform any of these actions on your phone, your device collects the information and prepares it to be sent over a network.

Transmitting Data over the Network
Data is moved over a wireless network

Once your phone has collected the data, it needs to send it over a network. This is where your cellular or Wi-Fi connection comes into play. When you send a message or make a call, your phone uses a wireless signal to send the data to the nearest cell tower or Wi-Fi router. From there, the data is sent over a series of interconnected networks until it reaches its destination.

Routing Data to the Data Center
Networking protocols route the data to a data center

As the data travels over the network, it needs to be routed to the correct destination, which in this case is the data center. This is done through a process called packet switching, where the data is broken down into smaller packets and sent through different routes to ensure it gets to its destination as quickly and efficiently as possible.

Storing Data in the Data Center
The data is stored on servers and devices in the data center

Once the data reaches the data center, it is stored on servers and other hardware that is designed to handlelarge amounts of data. The data center is essentially a massive storage facility that houses all kinds of data from various sources. When your data arrives at the data center, it is stored securely until it is needed again.

Processing Data in the Data Center
The data is processed by the computer systems using algorithms

Finally, the data is processed in the data center. This could involve anything from analyzing the data to extracting insights or running complex computations. The data center is equipped with powerful hardware and software that can handle these tasks quickly and efficiently.
